Ranch chicken tacos

Description

This recipe is a twist on your traditional taco Tuesday. If your family hufs and ouffs at the idea of tacos every week you can suprise them with these tasty and completly unique tacos.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of ranch dressing
  • 1/4 cup reduced-fat sour cream
  • 1 (1 ounce) packet taco seasoning mix, divided
  • 1 tablespoon mild chunky salsa
  • 2 cups shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 8 (6 inch) corn tortillas
  • shredded lettuce
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • 1(4 ounce) can slicedblack olives
  • 1 avacado-peeled, pitted and diced(optional)
  • 1 cup shredded Colby-Moterey Jack cheese

Steps

  1. In a small bowl combine ranch dressing, sour cream, 1 teaspoon of the taco seasoning, and salsa. Stir the ingredients and place the bowl in the refrigerator until ready to serve
  2. In a medium sized bowl add your chicken and the rest of the taco seasoning. Cover the bowl loosely with wax paper or plactic wrap. Microwave the chicken until it is heated through, roughly around 2-3 minutes.
  3. Warm your tortillas in a skillet for about one minute on each side.
  4. Place a scoop of chicken on the tortilla and top with your desiered topings. Don't forget the mixture that we placed in the refrigerator.
