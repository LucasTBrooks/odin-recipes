Asian lettuce wraps

Description

These are a intresting asian dish that everone will love. The lettuce wraps will allow peole to get their desired amount of filling. This dish takes a total of 30 minutes to make and can serve 4 people. The ingredients needed to make this dish will introduce you to new flvors and will peak your taste buds. From hoison sauce, minced pickled ginger, rice wine vinegar, Asian chile pepper sauce, and sesame oil these ingredientsare sure to get your taste buds watering.

Ingredients

  • 16 Boston Bibbs or butter lettuce leaves
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 large oinon, chopped
  • 2 cloves fresh garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon roce wine vinegar
  • 2 traspoons minched pickled ginger
  • 1 dash Asian chile pepper sauce, or to taste (Optional)
  • 1 bunch green onions, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons Asian (dark) sesame oil

Steps

  1. Rinse whole lettuce leaves and pat dry, being careful not to tear them; set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add beef;cook and stir until brown and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Drain and discard grease; transfer beef to a bowl. Add onion to the same skillet; cook and stir until just tender, 5 to 7 minutes.
  4. Stir in hoisin sauce, garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, ginger, and chile papper sauce until well combined.
  5. Add water chestnits, gree onions, sesame oil, and cooked beef; cook and stil until the onions just begin to wilt, about 2 minutes
  6. Arrange lettuce leaves around the outer edge of a large serving platter; spoon beef mixture in the center.
  7. Serve on small paltes or have napkins available since the mixture can sometimes drip.
